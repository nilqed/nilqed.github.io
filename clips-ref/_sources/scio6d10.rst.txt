LOOP-FOR-COUNT
==============

Allows simple iterative looping.

::

   (loop-for-count <range-spec> [do] <action>*)
   <range-spec>  ::= <end-index> |
                     (<loop-variable> [<start-index> <end-index>])
   <start-index> ::= <integer-expression>
   <end-index>   ::= <integer-expression>
