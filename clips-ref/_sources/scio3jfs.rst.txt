SWITCH
======

Allows a particular group of actions to be performed based on a
specified value.

::

   (switch <test-expression>
      <case-statement>*
      [<default-statement>])
   <case-statement> ::= (case <comparison-expression> then <action>*)
   <default-statement> ::= (default <action>*)
