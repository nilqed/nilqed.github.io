FiniteFieldNormalBasisExtension(GF, extdeg)

ffdoms.spad line 2699 [edit on github]

  • GF : FiniteFieldCategory
  • extdeg : PositiveInteger

FiniteFieldNormalBasisExtensionByPolynomial(GF, n) implements a finite extension field of degree n over the ground field GF. The elements are represented by coordinate vectors with respect to a normal basis, i.e. a basis consisting of the conjugates (q-powers) of an element, in this case called normal element. This is chosen as a root of the extension polynomial, created by createNormalPoly from FiniteFieldPolynomialPackage
